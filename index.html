Selected Works

Blackhead — Sound Design & Original Score
An exploration of heavy synthesis, raw instrumental distortion, and minimalist environmental textures. This project showcases an intricate deployment of acoustic dynamics and bespoke signal processing designed to build a tense, high-gravity narrative atmosphere that subverts traditional cinematic tropes.

Cadence Video Poetry Festival Showcase
Original architectural soundscapes and spatialized backing tracks featured and celebrated within this international showcase. This work highlights the intersection of avant-garde visual poetry and experimental audio processing systems.
